The new crown PCR test must be carried out at the airport upon arrival in Singapore, which is not applicable to ATP holders under the age of 12.
Children under the age of 12 who travel with their parents or guardians and are certified disabled or otherwise are not suitable for mobile devices.
Holders of Singapore citizens, permanent residents of Singapore and Long-Term Pass holders of Singapore do not have to apply for air travel permits from Chinese mainland to Singapore.
However, holders of long-term pass (LTP) still need to apply for entry approval from Singapore ICA before going to Singapore.
