Regarding the new crown polymerase reaction test that must be performed at the airport when arriving in Singapore, it is not applicable to ATP holders under the age of 12.
However, children under the age of 12 traveling with their parents or guardians and due to proven disabilities or other needs are not suitable for using mobile devices.
Singapore citizens, Singapore permanent residents and Singapore Long-Term Pass holders do not need to apply for an air travel permit from mainland China to Singapore.
However, Singapore Long-Term Pass (LTP) holders still need to apply for Entry Approval from Singapore ICA before going to Singapore.
